How to Play

You will have 30 seconds to answer the question on the board. Select an answer and click "Submit" to validate. Correct answers will turn green. Click "Next" to continue. Incorrect answers will turn red, and your game is over. You may quit at any time by clicking "Drop Out".

Cheats

The "Peek" will show you a possible answer, but you can decide whether you want to go with that answer.

The "Copy" will also show you an answer, however you are forced to take this answer as it will automatically be selected.

Let's Play!
